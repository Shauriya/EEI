About Us

The EEI is led by a team of high school students
originating from New Jersey, California, and Canada
that are passionate about business and social impact!

We are committed to empowering low-income students with the knowledge of financial literacy, design thinking, and economics to change the vicious cycle of poverty into a virtuous one!
